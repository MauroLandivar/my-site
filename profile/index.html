My Process

A structured 5-phase methodology from problem discovery to validated delivery.

01

Discovery

Empathize with users and stakeholders to uncover real problems before jumping to solutions.

02

Define

Prototyping and detailed description of the solution scope, requirements, and success criteria.

03

Estimate

Plan effort and timelines. Align teams on delivery expectations and resource allocation.

04

Develop

Build the solution iteratively with continuous collaboration between product, design, and engineering.

05

Deliver

Validate outcomes against goals, gather feedback, and iterate to maximize business impact.
